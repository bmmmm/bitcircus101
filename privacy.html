2. General Information

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR/DSGVO).

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Website Visits

When you visit our website, we collect the following technical data automatically:

  • IP address (anonymized after processing)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited
  • Referring website

3.2 Contact Information

When you contact us via email, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Message content

3.3 Donations

When making donations, third-party payment processors (PayPal, Ko-fi) collect and process payment information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): For website analytics and security
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): For cookies from third-party services
  • Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): For responding to inquiries

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Payment Processors

We use the following third-party payment processors:

  • PayPal: PayPal Privacy Policy
  • Ko-fi: Ko-fi Privacy Policy

5.2 Calendar Service

We embed a Nextcloud calendar (nc.6bm.de) for displaying events. This service may collect technical data when you view the calendar.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: For basic website functionality
  • Third-party cookies: From payment processors (only when using donation services)

We obtain your consent before setting non-essential cookies.

7. Data Retention

  • Technical data: Deleted after 30 days
  • Email correspondence: Retained for 3 years or until you request deletion
  • Donation records: Retained as required by German tax law (10 years)

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about your stored data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for secure data transmission.

10. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services may transfer your data outside the EU. These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses.
