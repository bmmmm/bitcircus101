About bitcircus101

bitcircus101 is a unique space located at Dorotheenstraße 101 in Bonn, Germany. We're designed to be a sanctuary where technology meets creativity. It's a place to relax and connect with like-minded individuals in a peaceful atmosphere.

Our space welcomes:

  • Tech enthusiasts looking for a quiet place to work or collaborate
  • Art lovers seeking inspiration and connection
  • People of all genders and backgrounds
  • Anyone seeking a calm environment with good internet connectivity

Whether you're working on your latest project, engaging in thoughtful conversations, or simply enjoying the peaceful atmosphere, bitcircus101 provides the perfect setting for your digital and creative endeavors in Bonn.

Linkup @ bitcircus101

Our monthly highlight: Linkup @ bitcircus101 takes place every third Friday of the month from 8 PM. Enjoy engaging lightning talks (3 slides, 3 minutes each), followed by relaxed hangout and tech conversations in a laid-back atmosphere.

Every other Friday from 8 PM, join us for Casual Linkup—bring your ideas, kick back with drinks & snacks, and connect with others in the tech community!
